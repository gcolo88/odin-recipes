Mate (drink)

Yerba mate, also known as "mate", is an herbal tea. It's a traditional drink in Latin and South America.

It's made by steeping dried leaves from the yerba mate plant in hot water.

Yerba mate can be served cold or hot.

Like black or green tea, it has caffeine, which can make you feel more alert and focused.

  6. Add hot water slowly on the side of the bombilla, until you see foam on the surface.
  7. As the yerba absorbs the water, pour a little more until it reaches the top, and your mate is ready to be enjoyed!
